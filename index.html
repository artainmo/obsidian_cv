Startup experience

Founding startup: Lowinsulin

April 2018 — January 2019

Lowinsulin helps type 2 diabetics reverse their condition and reduce medication costs by providing dietary guidance and enabling remote medication adjustments under the supervision of their physician.

I built a web platform to deliver dietary guidance and facilitate remote medication adjustments with physicians.

Founder Institute (Startup Incubator) with Lowinsulin

January 2019 — May 2019 | Luxembourg City, Luxembourg

The cohort started with 193 participants; after successive eliminations, only 10 participants graduated, myself among them.

I gained extensive insight into the startup creation process, covering areas such as marketing, legal administration, finance, hiring, management, product development, and scaling.

Birdhouse (Startup Accelerator) with Lowinsulin

May 2019 | Antwerpen, Belgium

Out of 650 startups, Lowinsulin advanced to the third round, among the remaining 70. Although not selected for the fourth round due to being at an early stage, the accelerator saw potential in the project and invited us to reapply in the next selection process.

Despite securing 12 B2B letters of intent to reach between 7000 and 7500 type 2 diabetics, I decided to step away from the startup due to limited technical experience at the time and chose to pursue further education at Campus 19.

Co-founding startup: Waffly

March 2021 — May 2021 | Leuven, Belgium

Waffly redefines the traditional waffle using local and sustainable ingredients while providing employment opportunities to individuals from disadvantaged backgrounds.

I joined, as leading ICT, what would become a team of 9. I learned more about team-work and business next to creating the digital marketplace.

Enactus national expo 2021 (Startup Competition) with Waffly
